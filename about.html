How We Began

A Simple Frustration, Turned Into a Brand

Beauty Pluz began with a simple frustration: skincare that promised the earth but read like a chemistry set. We wanted routines that respected both our skin and our time.

So we started formulating with a shorter, more honest list — botanical actives at clinical strength, nothing there to pad a label. Every product still has to earn its place in a five-step routine, or it doesn't ship.

"Slower formulas. Faster results. That's the whole idea."
